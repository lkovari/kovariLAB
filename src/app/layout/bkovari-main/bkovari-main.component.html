Bálint Kővári's page

Received a B.Sc. degree in vehicle engineering from Budapest University of Technology and Economics, Budapest, Hungary, in 2018, and an M.Sc. degree in Autonomous Vehicle Control Engineering in 2020. Since 2020, he has been a PhD. student at the Department of Control for Transportation and Vehicle Systems, Budapest University of Technology and Economics. His research interest includes artificial intelligence, machine learning, vehicle dynamics, and mechatronics.
